The Process

From kitchen to bowl in four steps

Every meal follows the same careful process: sourced that morning, cooked by hand, packed cold, and delivered to your door ready to serve.

01

Choose Your Plan

Select recipes, portion sizes, and a delivery cadence that fits your cat's appetite and your schedule.

02

We Source & Cook

Ingredients are sourced that morning from local purveyors. Your meals are hand-prepared in our licensed kitchen.

03

Packed & Shipped

Flash-frozen at peak freshness. Delivered in insulated, eco-friendly packaging with dry ice.

04

Serve & Store

Thaw in the fridge overnight, serve at room temperature. Stays fresh for 7 days refrigerated.
