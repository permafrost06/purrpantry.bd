Switching Your Cat to Purr Pantry

Many cats need time to adjust to a new food. A gradual transition helps prevent digestive upset and increases acceptance.

Day 1–2
25% Purr Pantry
75% Current Food
Day 3–4
50% Purr Pantry
50% Current Food
Day 5–6
75% Purr Pantry
25% Current Food
Day 7+
100% Purr Pantry
— All Done!

Tips for Success

  • ✔️ Warm slightly before serving
  • ✔️ Mix with familiar food initially
  • ✔️ Be patient with picky cats
  • ✔️ Offer at regular meal times
  • ✔️ Always provide fresh drinking water

For Dry Food Addicts 🍪

Some cats who have eaten only dry food for years may need extra time. Start with a small spoonful mixed into their regular food and increase gradually.

Patience is key.
